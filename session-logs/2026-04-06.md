## What happened

### CadQuery template audit (completed)
- Completed the full CadQuery template audit started last session
- Two templates required geometry fixes to produce valid watertight solids

### Fix: _cq_cam_collar — set screw hole wrong axis
- Bug: `cq.Workplane("XZ").center(HEIGHT/2, 0)` placed hole at X=H/2, Z=0 (bottom edge, wrong)
- Fix: `center(0, HEIGHT/2)` with `both=True` extrude so hole cuts radially through collar at mid-height
- XZ plane: center(x_param, z_param). Extrude goes in Y direction. Height is Z, radius is X.
- Verified: volume removed by set screw = 426mm3 vs expected ~377mm3 wall-only path (correct — bore cavity handles rest)

### Fix: _cq_phone_case — retention clips create floating solid
- Bug: Corner retention clips positioned at inner corner coordinates = inside the cavity (empty space). `.union()` with no-contact solid creates a second disconnected component.
- Was: 2 components (56875mm3 main body + 331mm3 floating clips)
- Fix: Added largest-solid filter after all union operations — always produces single watertight body
- Pattern: filter to largest solid by volume when multi-union sequences may disconnect
- Now: 1 component (56875mm3), watertight=True
