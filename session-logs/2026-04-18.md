## Key Decisions

- **Per-panel scroll vs outer column scroll**: outer-column scroll caused the PIPELINE LOG panel to flex-grow unpredictably, clipping content. Per-panel fixed heights (`maxHeight` on each Panel) give consistent visible areas and allow each section to scroll independently without affecting siblings.
- **Both DIY PCB routes in one module**: CNC isolation milling and 3D-printed channel fill are complementary — milling for rigid substrate, print-channel for rapid prototype. Keeping both in `diy_fab.py` avoids a proliferating `ecad/` module list and allows shared DRC checks and output contracts.
- **Cost estimator as separate endpoints**: `/api/cost/*` avoids coupling cost logic to the generation pipeline — cost can be queried independently (e.g., from the QuickBuilds panel before committing to a full run).
